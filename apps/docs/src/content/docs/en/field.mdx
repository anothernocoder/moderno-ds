Field wires accessibility, not appearance: Ark's Field machine associates
`label[for]` with the control's `id` and mirrors `aria-invalid`/
`aria-describedby`/`aria-required` onto `data-invalid`/`data-disabled`/
`data-required` — those attributes, not CVA variants, are what
`components.css` keys the error/disabled look off.

<CodeBlock
  code={`<Field.Root invalid={hasError}>
  <Field.Label>Email</Field.Label>
  <Field.Input type="email" />
  <Field.ErrorText>Enter a valid email.</Field.ErrorText>
</Field.Root>`}
/>
